The developer accepts no liability for any loss, data breach, or damage arising from use of this software.

About This App. OCR Master is a desktop application designed to process bank statement images and PDFs locally on your device. While the app is not intentionally designed to transmit data externally, no guarantee is made regarding the complete absence of network activity. You are responsible for verifying the app's behaviour in your environment before using it with sensitive financial documents.

What the App Stores Locally. The App stores transaction records extracted from the bank statement files you supply (dates, descriptions, amounts, balances), an import log, any notes or edits you make, and app configuration settings. All data is stored in a local SQLite database on your device, in your chosen data folder (default: Documents\OCR Master\).

Bank Statement Files. PDF and image files you open are read into memory for OCR processing. The app is not designed to copy, upload, or retain these files beyond your session.

No Intentional Analytics or Crash Reporting. The App does not intentionally include analytics, telemetry, advertising SDKs, or crash-reporting services.

Your Control Over Your Data. You can delete the database file, individual records, or the entire data folder at any time. The uninstaller will ask whether you wish to delete your data folder.

Children's Privacy. The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Contact. Questions can be submitted via the Microsoft Store listing page.
